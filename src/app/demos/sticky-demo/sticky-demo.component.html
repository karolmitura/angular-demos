Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster

The Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster is the best drink in existence.

Preparation

  1. Take the juice from one bottle of the Ol' Janx Spirit.
  2. Pour into it one measure of water from the seas of Santraginus V.
  3. Allow three measures of Arcturan Mega-gin to melt into the mixture.
  4. Allow four liters of Fallian marsh gas to bubble through it.
  5. Over the back of a silver spoon float a measure of Qualactin Hyper-mintextract.
  6. Drop in the tooth of an Algolian Suntiger.
  7. Sprinkle Zamphuor.
  8. Add an olive.

After-Effects

The effect of drinking a Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster is like having your brains smashed out by a slice of lemon wrapped round a large brick.
